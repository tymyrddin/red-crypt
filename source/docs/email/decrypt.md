# Decrypt the message itself

## Attack tree

```text
1 Break asymmetric encryption (OR)
    1.1 Brute force break asymmetric encryption (OR)
    1.2 Mathematically break asymmetric encryption (OR)
        1.2.1 Break RSA (OR)
        1.2.2 Factor RSA modulus/calculate ElGamal discrete log 
    1.3 Cryptanalyse asymmetric encryption
        1.3.1 General Cryptanalysis of RSA/ElGamal (OR)
        1.3.2 Exploiting weaknesses in RSA/ElGamal (OR)
        1.3.3 Timing attacks on RSA/ElGamal
2 Break symmetric key encryption
    2.1 Brute force break symmetric key encryption (OR)
    2.2 Cryptanalyse symmetric key encryption
```
